### Gate Approvals (1.5 minutes)

**Navigate to:** Gate Approval modal (click "Complete Phase" or use demo trigger)

> "Here's where Solo Mode shines. Traditional Shipley requires multiple executives to approve gates. In Solo Mode, the operator makes the call—but they're not flying blind."

**Show:** AI recommendation panel

> "The AI agents analyze your work and provide a recommendation: Go, Conditional Go, Pause, or No-Go. They explain their reasoning."

**Point to:** AI confidence indicators (green/yellow/red)

> "Green means high confidence—the AI thinks you're ready. Yellow means review recommended. Red means there are issues to address."

**Action:** Click "GO" to approve the gate

> "The operator makes the final decision. If they disagree with the AI, they can override it—but they have to document why. Everything is logged for audit purposes."

**Q: How is this different from just giving someone all permissions?**

Gate reviews become self-assessments with AI guidance. It's a different mental model, not just a permission change."
