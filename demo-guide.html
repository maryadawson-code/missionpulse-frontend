10-Minute Demo Script

Talk track for live demos and recorded walkthroughs

0:00 - 1:00 | OPENING

The Problem

"Most GovCon teams run proposals out of spreadsheets, email chains, and shared drives. It takes 520+ hours per proposal, and win rates sit at 30-40%. MissionPulse changes that."

▶ Stay on login screen. Let the branding speak.

1:00 - 2:30 | DASHBOARD

Command Center Overview

"After login, you land on your command center. Pipeline value, active opportunities, average win probability -- all in real time. Every module is role-filtered. Your CEO sees Black Hat analysis. Your volume leads see only what they need."

▶ Log in as CEO. Point out the role badge and module grid. Click a module card.

2:30 - 4:00 | PIPELINE

Opportunity Pipeline

"Every pursuit lives here. Stage tracking, win probability, ceiling values. Filter by agency, priority, or stage. One click to export to CSV for your weekly pipeline review."

▶ Show pipeline-kanban. Filter by stage. Click Export CSV.

4:00 - 5:30 | COMPLIANCE

Compliance Matrix

"RFP requirements mapped to your response sections. Red/yellow/green status tracking. Export to Word for your compliance appendix -- formatted, ready to submit."

▶ Show compliance-checker. Add a requirement. Export Word doc. Open in Word.

5:30 - 6:30 | COMPETITOR INTEL

Competitive Intelligence

"Know your competitors before they know you. Strengths, weaknesses, threat levels, contract history. This feeds directly into your Black Hat review -- which only executive roles can access."

▶ Show competitor-intel. Point out threat levels. Mention RBAC restriction on Black Hat.

6:30 - 7:30 | SECTION WRITER

Proposal Section Writer

"Your volume leads write sections here. Status tracking per section. When ready, one click exports the entire technical volume as a formatted Word document with your company header and footer."

▶ Show section-writer. Click Export Word. Open the .doc file.

7:30 - 8:30 | PRICING

BOE Builder (CUI-Protected)

"Basis of Estimate builder with CUI markings baked in. Labor categories, rates, justifications. Export is restricted to roles with CUI clearance -- the system enforces it, not your honor system."

▶ Show boe-builder. Point out CUI banner. Show that export is role-gated.

8:30 - 9:30 | BILLING

Plans + Onboarding

"14-day free trial, full access. Starter at $499/month for small shops, Professional at $999 for full teams. Self-service upgrade through Stripe."

▶ Show billing.html. Point out usage meters and plan comparison.

9:30 - 10:00 | CLOSE

The Ask

"MissionPulse cuts proposal time by 35% and gives your team a single source of truth. Ready to start your free trial?"

▶ Open signup.html. Walk through the 30-second signup flow.
