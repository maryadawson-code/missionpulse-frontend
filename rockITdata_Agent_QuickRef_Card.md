## ROLE CHECK (First Question Every Time)
> "Are we PRIME or SUB? If SUB, who is the Prime?"

- **PRIME:** "rockITdata will deliver..." → FAR 52.219-14 is YOUR responsibility
- **SUB:** "As a teammate to [Prime], rockITdata provides..." → Stay in your lane
