PERFORMANCE WORK STATEMENT (PWS)

Solicitation Number: N00014-26-R-9999
Project Title: OPERATION GLASS ONION: Enterprise Data Synchronization & Logistics (EDSL)
Agency: Department of Administrative Logistics (DAL)

================================================================================
1.0 BACKGROUND
================================================================================

The Department of Administrative Logistics (DAL) requires a "Digital Twin" solution to modernize the legacy AS-400 mainframe systems currently managing the inventory of 4.5 million widget units. The contractor shall provide all personnel, equipment, supplies, facilities, transportation, tools, materials, supervision, and other items and non-personal services necessary to perform the work.

================================================================================
2.0 SCOPE OF WORK
================================================================================

The Contractor shall migrate all data from the legacy COBOL environment to a cloud-native architecture.

  * Constraint A: The new system must be fully operational by September 30, 2026.
  
  * Constraint B: The Contractor is prohibited from accessing the legacy source code until October 1, 2026 due to pending security reviews.

================================================================================
3.0 SPECIFIC TASKS
================================================================================

3.1 Task 1 - Data Ingestion

The contractor shall ingest unstructured data formats including .TIFF, .CSV, and handwritten .PDF manifests.

  * Note: Accuracy must be 99.9%.
  * Exception: For historical files pre-1990, accuracy of 50% is acceptable if marked with "legacy" flag.

3.2 Task 2 - Key Personnel

The Government requires the following Key Personnel. Resumes must be submitted with the proposal.

  1. Program Manager: PMP required. 10 years experience.
  2. Sr. Developer: Must have 5 years of experience in "Rust" and "Fortran."

¹ Notwithstanding the above, the Sr. Developer must actually hold a Top Secret/SCI clearance at time of award, though this is not mentioned in the main bullet point.
