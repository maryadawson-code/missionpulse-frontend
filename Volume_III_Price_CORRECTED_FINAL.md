## 1. PRICING STRATEGY & METHODOLOGY 

### 1.1 Strategic Approach: Independent Governance Partner 

rockITdata submits this Firm Fixed Price (FFP) proposal in response to Defense Health Agency (DHA) Solicitation HT001126RE011 for Data Governance Services. As an Independent Governance Partner with no ownership stake in proprietary data fabrics, hosting contracts, or catalog platforms, rockITdata provides neutral oversight between Agency mission owners, incumbent engineering vendors, and commercial catalog providers. Rather than a traditional "linear staffing" approach, we have engineered an Optimized Resource Model powered by our AMANDA™ Framework that front-loads expertise to tackle the initial inventory surge, then right-sizes headcount as our automated harvesting pipelines (Objective 4) replace manual data entry tasks. 

This approach delivers a competitive price while maintaining the senior-level talent required for high-complexity systems. Our pricing directly traces to a detailed Work Breakdown Structure (WBS), Integrated Master Schedule (IMS), and 10 verified decision gates—providing full "golden thread" traceability from technical approach to cost. 

### 1.2 The Remote Advantage: Strategic Cost Positioning 

rockITdata leverages a secure distributed workforce per Q&A #23 hybrid/remote authorization. By performing non-classified work remotely, we avoid the heavy overhead multipliers associated with National Capital Region (NCR) facilities. This allows us to provide Senior-Level Talent at rates comparable to Junior-Level Onsite Staff, delivering higher value per dollar while maintaining full availability during DHA core hours. Only the Program Manager, Technical Lead, and Sr. Data Governance SME are Hybrid (NCR) for required on-site deep-dives and quarterly reviews. 

Our proposed rates are competitive with multiple independent benchmarks. GSA Schedule 70 IT Professional Services rates for comparable NCR positions range from $165-$225/hour for Program Managers and $140-$190/hour for Senior SMEs. Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS) Occupational Employment Statistics for the Washington-Arlington-Alexandria metropolitan area show median hourly wages of $75-$95 for Computer and Information Analysts (SOC 15-1211) and $55-$75 for Computer Systems Analysts (SOC 15-1212). rockITdata's remote positioning enables rates 15-25% below NCR GSA benchmarks while maintaining equivalent qualifications and exceeding BLS medians to ensure talent retention.
