## 1. WHAT IS SOLO MODE?

Solo Mode transforms MissionPulse into a personal proposal command center for individuals and small teams. Instead of coordinating across 11 different Shipley roles, Solo Mode consolidates everything under a single "Solo Owner" role with AI agents serving as your virtual team members.

**Key Benefits:**

- **One-Person Operations:** Manage the complete capture-to-submission lifecycle yourself
- **AI Virtual Team:** 8 specialized AI agents act as your Capture Manager, Pricing Analyst, Compliance Officer, and more
- **Guided Workflow:** Step-by-step Shipley methodology with built-in checkpoints
- **Self-Approval Gates:** Make go/no-go decisions with AI recommendations at each milestone
- **Simplified Interface:** Streamlined UI without the complexity of multi-team coordination

## 2. WHO SHOULD USE SOLO MODE

Solo Mode is designed for:

| Profile | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Solo BD Professionals** | Independent consultants who manage pursuits end-to-end |
| **Small Business Owners** | Companies with 1-10 employees without dedicated proposal staff |
| **Startup GovCon Firms** | New federal contractors building their first proposals |
| **Part-Time Proposalists** | Staff who handle proposals alongside other duties |

**Solo Mode is NOT recommended for:**

- Teams with 5+ dedicated proposal staff
- Organizations with established Shipley review processes
- Proposals requiring formal color team reviews with external stakeholders

## 3. GETTING STARTED

### 3.1 Enabling Solo Mode

Solo Mode requires a Professional or Enterprise subscription. Here's how to enable it:

1. Navigate to **Admin Settings** (gear icon in top-right)
2. Select the **Features** tab
3. Locate "Solo Proposal Manager Mode"
4. Toggle the switch to **Enabled**
5. Click **Save Changes**

[SCREENSHOT: Admin Settings page with Solo Mode toggle highlighted]

> **Note:** Only users with Executive, Operations, or Admin roles can enable Solo Mode for the organization.

### 3.2 Starting a Solo Proposal

Once Solo Mode is enabled:

1. Click **+ New Proposal** from any screen
2. Select "Start in Solo Mode"
3. Enter the opportunity name and upload your PWS/SOW (optional)
4. Click **Begin Discovery Phase**

[SCREENSHOT: New proposal modal with "Start in Solo Mode" option]

### 3.3 The Solo Mode Interface

The Solo Mode interface is organized around a horizontal timeline showing your progress through all 6 Shipley phases:

- **Phase Indicator:** Shows your current phase and completion percentage
- **AI Panel:** Quick access to your virtual team members
- **Action Queue:** Tasks recommended by AI for your current phase
- **Gate Status:** Visual indicator of upcoming approval gates

## 4. THE 6 SHIPLEY PHASES

Solo Mode guides you through the complete Shipley methodology in 6 distinct phases.

### Phase 1: Discovery (Pre-RFP)

**Objective:** Understand the opportunity and gather intelligence

**Activities:**
- Research the customer and their mission
- Identify incumbent contractors and their performance
- Map stakeholders and decision-makers
- Assess initial fit with your capabilities

**AI Assistance:** The Capture Agent helps you research the customer, identify key stakeholders, and assess opportunity fit.

**Gate Approval:** Discovery Complete → Proceed to Capture Planning?

---

### Phase 2: Capture Planning

**Objective:** Develop your win strategy

**Activities:**
- Define your win themes and discriminators
- Identify teaming partners (if needed)
- Build competitive assessment (Black Hat analysis)
- Estimate pricing range and B&P budget

**AI Assistance:** The Strategy Agent and Black Hat Agent help develop competitive positioning while the Pricing Agent provides preliminary cost estimates.

**Gate Approval:** Capture Plan Complete → Proceed to RFP Analysis?

---

### Phase 3: RFP Analysis

**Objective:** Deconstruct the solicitation requirements

**Activities:**
- Parse PWS/SOW requirements into compliance matrix
- Identify evaluation criteria and weights
- Map required certifications and experience
- Flag clarification questions

**AI Assistance:** The Compliance Agent auto-generates your compliance matrix and identifies potential gaps in your qualifications.

**Gate Approval:** RFP Analysis Complete → Proceed to Proposal Planning?

---

### Phase 4: Proposal Planning

**Objective:** Structure your response

**Activities:**
- Create proposal outline aligned to evaluation criteria
- Assign content sections to yourself (or AI drafting)
- Set internal deadlines for each section
- Gather past performance references

**AI Assistance:** The Writer Agent creates section outlines while the Orals Agent prepares presentation templates if oral presentations are required.

**Gate Approval:** Proposal Plan Complete → Begin Production?

---

### Phase 5: Production

**Objective:** Write and assemble the proposal

**Activities:**
- Draft technical approach sections
- Complete staffing and management approach
- Finalize pricing spreadsheets and narratives
- Compile and format all volumes

**AI Assistance:** The Writer Agent drafts sections based on your outlines. The Pricing Agent validates your BOE calculations. The Compliance Agent performs continuous compliance checks.

**Gate Approval:** Draft Complete → Proceed to Final Review?

---

### Phase 6: Final Review

**Objective:** Quality assurance and submission

**Activities:**
- Final compliance check against all requirements
- Proofread all sections for consistency
- Validate all pricing calculations
- Package for submission

**AI Assistance:** All agents perform final checks in their domains. The system generates a "Submission Readiness Score" with actionable recommendations.

**Gate Approval:** Final Review Complete → Approve for Submission?

## 5. WORKING WITH AI AGENTS

### 5.1 Your Virtual Team

In Solo Mode, AI agents function as your virtual team members, each specializing in a Shipley role:

| Agent | Virtual Role | Primary Functions |
|-------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Capture Agent** | Capture Manager | Opportunity assessment, customer research, win probability |
| **Strategy Agent** | Strategy Lead | Win themes, discriminators, competitive positioning |
| **Black Hat Agent** | Competitive Intel | Competitor analysis, threat assessment |
| **Writer Agent** | Proposal Writer | Section drafting, content generation |
| **Compliance Agent** | Contracts Specialist | FAR/DFARS compliance, requirement mapping |
| **Pricing Agent** | Pricing Analyst | BOE development, rate validation |
| **Contracts Agent** | Legal Reviewer | Contract terms, risk identification |
| **Orals Agent** | Presentation Coach | Slide development, Q&A preparation |

### 5.2 Requesting AI Assistance

To engage an AI agent:

1. Click the **AI Assistant** icon (chat bubble) in the bottom-right corner
2. Select the appropriate agent from the dropdown
3. Describe your task or question
4. Review the AI-generated response
5. Accept, modify, or regenerate the output

[SCREENSHOT: AI chat panel showing agent selection dropdown]

### 5.3 AI Recommendations

Throughout each phase, AI agents proactively suggest actions in your Action Queue:

- **Green recommendations:** High confidence, proceed with AI suggestion
- **Yellow recommendations:** Moderate confidence, review recommended
- **Red flags:** Issues requiring your attention before proceeding

> **Important:** All AI-generated content displays the disclaimer: "AI GENERATED - REQUIRES HUMAN REVIEW"

## 6. GATE APPROVALS

### 6.1 Understanding Gates

Gate Approvals are self-assessment checkpoints that occur between each Shipley phase. They ensure you've completed the necessary activities before moving forward.

**Solo Mode simplifies the traditional multi-person gate review into a self-approval with AI guidance.**

### 6.2 The Gate Approval Process

At each gate:

1. **Review Checklist:** System presents a checklist of completed activities
2. **AI Assessment:** Your AI agents provide recommendations (Go/Conditional Go/Pause/No-Go)
3. **Your Decision:** You make the final call to proceed, pause, or stop
4. **Audit Trail:** Your decision is logged with timestamp and rationale

[SCREENSHOT: Gate Approval modal showing AI recommendations and decision buttons]

### 6.3 Gate Decisions

| Decision | Meaning |
|----------|---------|
| **GO** | Proceed to next phase with full confidence |
| **CONDITIONAL GO** | Proceed with documented caveats or risks |
| **PAUSE** | Hold until specific items are resolved |
| **NO-GO** | Discontinue this pursuit |

### 6.4 Overriding AI Recommendations

You are always the final decision-maker. If you disagree with AI recommendations:

1. Click "Override AI Recommendation"
2. Enter your rationale for the decision
3. The system logs your override with justification
4. You proceed with your chosen path

## 7. SWITCHING BETWEEN MODES

### 7.1 From Solo Mode to Team Mode

If your opportunity grows and requires team support:

1. Open the proposal in Solo Mode
2. Click **Proposal Settings** (gear icon)
3. Select "Convert to Team Mode"
4. Assign the 11 Shipley roles to team members
5. All data and progress transfers automatically

### 7.2 From Team Mode to Solo Mode

If you need to take over a team proposal as a solo operator:

1. Open the proposal in Team Mode
2. Click **Proposal Settings** (gear icon)
3. Select "Convert to Solo Mode"
4. Confirm that you will assume all role responsibilities
5. You become the Solo Owner with full access

> **Warning:** Converting to Solo Mode consolidates all role permissions under your account. Previous role assignments are archived in the audit log.

## 8. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### General Questions

**Q: Who is Solo Mode for?**

A: Solo Mode is designed for individual BD professionals, small businesses (1-10 employees), and companies without dedicated proposal staff. It's ideal for operators who manage pursuits independently.

**Q: What subscription tier is required for Solo Mode?**

A: Solo Mode requires a Professional or Enterprise subscription. Starter plans do not include Solo Mode.

**Q: Can I switch between Solo Mode and Team Mode?**

A: Yes. You can convert any proposal between Solo Mode and Team Mode at any time. All data and progress transfers automatically.

### Data & Security

**Q: What happens to my data if I disable Solo Mode?**

A: Your data remains intact. Disabling Solo Mode simply hides the Solo Mode interface; all proposals, documents, and audit logs are preserved and accessible in Team Mode.

**Q: Is CUI still protected in Solo Mode?**

A: Yes. All CUI protections, automatic classification, and audit logging apply equally in Solo Mode. The system routes CUI-containing queries to FedRAMP High authorized systems regardless of mode.

### AI Agents

**Q: Do AI agents work the same in Solo Mode?**

A: Yes. You have access to all 8 AI agents in Solo Mode. The difference is that you interact with them as your "virtual team" rather than as assistants to specific role holders.

**Q: Can I override AI recommendations?**

A: Absolutely. AI agents provide recommendations, but you are always the final decision-maker. All overrides are logged with your rationale for audit purposes.

### Billing & Subscription

**Q: Does Solo Mode cost extra?**

A: Solo Mode is included in Professional and Enterprise subscriptions at no additional cost.

**Q: Can multiple users at my company use Solo Mode?**

A: Yes. Each user can independently enable Solo Mode for their proposals. This is useful for companies with multiple BD professionals working separate pursuits.
