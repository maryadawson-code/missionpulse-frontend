Solo Proposal Manager Guide

What is Solo Mode?

Solo Mode is designed for small businesses where one person handles the entire proposal process. The AI acts as your virtual team, providing analysis and drafts for each Shipley phase.

How it Works

  1. Select or create an opportunity
  2. Work through each phase's tasks with AI assistance
  3. At each gate, review AI recommendations and make your decision
  4. Progress through all 6 phases to submission

CUI Protection

The system automatically detects Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI) in your queries and routes them to FedRAMP High authorized systems. You'll see a yellow banner when this occurs.
